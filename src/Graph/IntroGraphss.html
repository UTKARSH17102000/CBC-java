Graph - Data Structrue
Components - Nodes/Vertex and Edge(Connects Two Nodes)
Types - Undirected Graphs(Edges are not Directed in a particular Direction) & Directed Graphs(Directed Edges)
Degree -No. of edges which incoming and ongoing
Property of degree in Undirected graphs -

Total degree of all the nodes is twice of all edges


Directeg Graphs - Two types of degree Indegree(Edges coming to the Node) and Outdegree(Edges going out of the node)
Path(Undirected) - Sequence of nodes such that no node visited twice
Path(Directed) - Same as undirected just moves in a particular directions of edges
Cyclic - It Means if u are standing on a particular node u can reach back to that node using somo different path .
Acylic - No cycle present in the graph .
DAG - Directed Acyclic Graph
Weights - value of an edge between two nodes .
